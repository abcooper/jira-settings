JIRA-Helper Options

Custom Field Links

Click the label of a custom field to open its contents in a new tab. (Useful for fields with poorly styled tables)

Customizable Statuses

Customize the names of workflow statuses that are used by mapping the current name to the desired name.

Open PDFs in Browser

Clicking the link to a PDF will open it in a new tab, rather than downloading it. (Note: this will be applied to all pages, not just JIRA)
